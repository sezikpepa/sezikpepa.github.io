Me as a learner

Whenever I study or do assignments I do it spontaneously, I do not plan a concrete structure of my day or week, but I rather do tasks when I feel I am in correct mood for that work. Moreover, with approaching deadlines I dedicate more time to that specific task. I can see some flaws in this technique, but so far I worked great for me. Everytime I do something, I do it on 100 %, so I finish my work on time but I need specific deadlines because the pressure of time gives me motivation to continue on the work.

Best techniques which always worked for me during studying sessions is studying with visual aids. With a grain of salt, whenever I see something I will never forget it, because I see because the layout, colors and patterns will always stick in my head. This includes, graphs, videos, picture and many other forms. To learn practical things I always stand for opinion that to learn specific task it is necessary to do that specific task. For example it is not possible to learn how to programm by listening to lectures about programming, but it is important to sit behind the computer and try to make a computer program and use lectures and videos as a source of help when new problem arrise during programming.

Thought process behind selection of learning goals

As I am studying my master degree, I have experienced different situations which helped me to shift my direction toward my dream job. I am still a student but I have a quite clear vision about my future but there are still loose ends about specific details and there are always things which could be improved regarthless the journey.

After these few weeks of studing at Maastricht University I realized that the university is completly different than my university in the Czech Republic. I am not saying it is better or worse, just different. Main difference lays in the teaching process, it is not about "how" but about "what". During my bachelor I learned a lot of hard skills in the mathematical and computer science field. I can feel that Maastricht University teaches the other part. What do I mean by this sentence? The focus is more on communication a cooperation in a given environment, specifically for example - how to influence different people, communication with stakeholders, how to gain an advantage of that. I have realized that these are also important skills and I did not give them a lot of attention as I have not thought about them. To address this "problem" I visited "Discover your competencies" workshop held by Carreer Centrum at Maastricht University to learn more about skills I know, but moreover to discover things I could know but I do not. Workshop pointed out information about whose I was aware of. I knew that I enjoy technical problems and this field but it forced me to think about which section of this field I would like to pursue. During the converstations with particiappnts of the workshop it was pointed out that I could probably be able to explain topics to other people
